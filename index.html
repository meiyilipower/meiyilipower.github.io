Brief Biography

I am a graduate student in Electrical Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU). I hold a B.S. degree in Outstanding Engineers Honor Class, Electrical Engineering and Automation, SJTU. I work on various problems in modeling, control and stability analysis of distributed generators.
